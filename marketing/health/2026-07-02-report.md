## Top 3 Marketing Highlights This Week

> These are the features that represent OpenTutor's strongest differentiation and are ready to showcase.

1. **Coding Question Type with LLM Grading** — OpenTutor can generate and auto-grade coding exercises from uploaded course material. This is a genuine differentiator versus Anki, Quizlet, or NotebookLM and maps to a large audience of CS/engineering learners. A short demo video showing "upload a Python tutorial PDF → get a graded coding quiz" would go viral in dev-learning communities.

2. **Local-First with 10+ LLM Providers** — The zero-API-key onboarding story (Ollama default, bring-your-own key optional) is the #1 privacy and cost differentiator. Most AI tutors require cloud accounts. The `3 commands` Quick Start in the README is the strongest marketing asset in the repo — it should be the lead in every post.

3. **Dashboard Weekly Stats + Mastery Overview** — The analytics features (learning progress, forgetting forecasts, FSRS scheduling) give OpenTutor a "quantified self for learning" angle. Screenshots of the mastery overview and retention forecast are high-shareability visuals for Twitter/Reddit.
