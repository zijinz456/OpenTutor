### 3. LECTOR Semantic Spaced Repetition (shipped, under-promoted)

It appears in the README but has **never been the subject of a build-in-public post or community post**. This is the biggest untapped story. Audience: Anki users, learning science community, ML researchers interested in applied spaced repetition.
