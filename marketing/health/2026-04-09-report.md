### 2. The Mastery Timeline Dashboard — A Shareable Social Visual

The dashboard upgrade (weekly stats, mastery overview, summary block, timeline view) produces the kind of screen that learners screenshot and share. ### 3. Cognitive Load Detection — The Learning Science Story

The block unlock overlay and cognitive load tuning features enable a unique editorial angle: *"We read the research papers so you don't have to."* OpenTutor's implementation of cognitive load theory (12-signal detection, behavioral adaptation, progressive feature unlock) is grounded in a [Nature 2025 paper](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41598-025-10497-x) and differentiates the product from every LLM wrapper calling itself an "AI tutor."

**Suggested editorial format:** A 500-word blog post or Twitter thread explaining the research → implementation story. Non-technical learners will share "AI that detects when you're tired" headlines; technical readers will engage with the behavioral signal architecture.
